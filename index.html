How it works

Three steps to tidy contacts.

01

Tap Scan Contacts and allow access when prompted. Standard iOS permissions — you're in control.

02

Review the changes

ContactFixer shows you every number it plans to update. Scroll through and verify before committing.

One tap updates all contacts instantly via the native iOS Contacts framework.
